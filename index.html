  About Me


I'm a creative and dedicated Graphic Design and Animation enthusiast currently studying at Saint Paul College, MN. I love turning ideas into visuals using software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Blender. From making cool graphics to bringing stories to life through animation, I enjoy every step of the process.
I'm a team player who listens well and pays attention to detail. Whether I'm working alone or with others, I'm committed to doing my best and delivering great results. I'm always learning new things to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in design and animation.
I'm excited about the chance to use my skills to create awesome visual experiences that people will love. With my passion for creativity and my drive to succeed, I'm ready to make a positive impact wherever I go.
